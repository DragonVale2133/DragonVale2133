Treasure Box Guide

The Treasure Box is a feature that rewards users when they click the Daycare everyday, without missing a single one.

These are given out on reset at Monday every week, which can then be opened by going to your dashboard (Player, or by clicking your profile).

In order to earn one, you have to click the daycare or any amount of babies to get shards. Collect 7 and you meet the quota for the day.


Want to know what colors you can get from a box? Click the link that's under Outcome on the left and that is marked with CURRENT, or click here.
