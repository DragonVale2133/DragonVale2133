Species List - Equines

All horses, except for the Hippocampi, hatch at level 75 and grow up at level 150. Excluding same-sex species, these species can breed (no cross-breeding though!), however some colors may not be able to breed due to reasons like them being event-only colors.

Unicorn

Arguably the most common horse species. Unicorns have three adult poses, though the third one has yet to be released yet.


Pegasus

Like the Unicorn, there are three adult linearts, however the Pegasus' is already out for a good while now. It can only be gotten through either events or the Gold Shop. Other than that, also a common species, probably as common as the Unicorn.


Alicorn

A mix of the Pegasus and Unicorn. Used to be one of the rarer species of the Valley, but due to a few changes they're now profound in the Valley as well. Alicorns only have one adult version.


Spring Fairy

Not as widespread as the three above, though they are more frequently found than the same-sex species. A Spring Fairy EY is also called a Caterpony.


Heraldic Unicorn

One of the three same-sex species of the Valley. Due to them all being mares, they cannot breed. Heraldic Unicorns have a longer horn and a short mane. Eternal Youth Heraldics were introduced in Winter 2014.


Paaefarin

One of the three same-sex species of the Valley. Due to them all being stallions, they cannot breed. Paaefarins bear a resemblence to Gypsy Vanner horses. Eternal Youth Paaefarins were introduced in Winter 2014.


Fakiecorn

One of the three same-sex species of the Valley. Due to them all being mares, they cannot breed. Fakiecorns from the Anniversary event can have different party hats and blowers, while Ebil Fakies may have different headgear like candles.
